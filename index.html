About SpeakWithEbere

We provide a readily-accessible individual counselling/psychological therapy with psychological professionals and mental health social workers for people with, or at the risk of developing a mental illness.

The rate of suicide in Nigeria, and in the world at large, today is alarming especially in recent years, hence the establishment of SpeakWithEbere to curb this menace. We provide a very agreeable and conducive platform for individuals to reach out and communicate their negative feelings and unflattering emotions to sympathetic mental health social workers who would help in re-conditioning their thinking, and to see, all over again, great worth in themselves.

Our Values

MISSION: We are set to provide an enabling environment for indivuals to reach out, without any fear of being judged, whenever they feel less than they should. We are most certainly about creating a society where every individual knows his/her total worth, and understands that their value is not tied to the opinions of others. Apart from readily coming to the rescue of users who reach out, surepsyche also provides awareness and education to the unsuspecting who might be unaware that they are truly mentally ill and need help and assistance.

VISION: SpeakWithEbere is set out to rid the society, one person at a time, of depression and other mental health conditions, especially those borne out of inferiority complex, negative circumstances, etc. We seek to create in the nearest future, a world that is, to a very large extent, rid of actual successful suicides and suicidal attempts. We are about creating a society made up of people who love themselves absolutely and are a hundred percent confident in their worth and abilities.
